The Annual scientific Conference

We are thrilled that you are considering being a part of it. Attending the Baghdad Derma Annual scientific Conference is a richer, more immersive learning experience than any other in the aesthetic medical world , more than 300 participants , 10 expert speakers and 12 exhibiting companies are what will make this gathering of the greatest minds in medical aesthetic a landmark event, setting the bar for the rest of the profession in 2017.

The scientific program for 2017 has been carefully crafted to incorporate all aspects of plastic surgery. Dermatology and medical aesthetic practice, Essential topics such as body and face surgery, injectables, suspending devices, clinical dermatology and lasers & EDB will all be covered over the course of the four days. We strive year after year to expand the scope of the field to include sessions in clinical dermatology, cosmeceuticals, hair restoration, ethnic morphologies and professional business in order to reflect the breadth and complexity of our field. As those that have come before it, this 1st edition of the Baghdad Derma congress will open with a bang with the world - famous anatomy via live stream for the most in-depth, detailed insights into procedures.
